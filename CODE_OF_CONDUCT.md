# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

Participation in this project should be respectful, constructive, and professional.

Examples of expected behavior:

- giving useful, specific technical feedback
- assuming good faith
- being respectful of different experience levels
- focusing discussion on the code, design, and observed behavior

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- harassment, insults, or personal attacks
- discriminatory or exclusionary language
- bad-faith disruption of discussions or reviews
- publishing private information without permission

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to project spaces, including issues, pull requests, code review, and other public interactions around the repository.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, and other contributions that do not follow this Code of Conduct.

Repeated or severe violations may lead to temporary or permanent exclusion from the project.

## Reporting

Report conduct issues privately to the maintainers through the security contact listed in [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md).

All reports will be reviewed and handled in good faith.
